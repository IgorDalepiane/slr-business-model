# Research Questions Context

## Overview
10 research questions (RQ01-RQ10) guide the SLR. 33 extraction questions (EQ01-EQ33) map to these RQs. 100% data coverage.

## Research Questions (RQ01-RQ10)

**RQ01**: Business Model Identification and Taxonomy  
What business models have been identified, and how are they defined, classified, and differentiated?

**RQ02**: Application Context and Market Segmentation  
In which contexts (verticals, customer types, geographies, company sizes) are models implemented, and what patterns emerge?

**RQ03**: Technical and Architectural Characteristics  
What technical, architectural, and delivery characteristics distinguish models, and how do they influence monetization?

**RQ04**: Monetization and Pricing Strategies  
What monetization and pricing strategies are used, and how do they relate to model and market characteristics?

**RQ05**: Ecosystem Dynamics and Go-to-Market  
What ecosystem dynamics (roles, relationships, network effects) and go-to-market strategies (channels, marketplaces) are associated with models?

**RQ06**: Motivations, Success Factors, and Adoption Conditions  
What are adoption motivations, success factors, and necessary conditions for successful implementation?

**RQ07**: Implementation Challenges and Mitigation  
What are the main implementation challenges, and what effective mitigation practices have been used?

**RQ08**: Temporal Evolution and Future Trends  
What is the temporal evolution and maturity of models, and what are future trends and projected directions?

**RQ09**: Business Model Canvas Elements  
What are the distinctive characteristics and BMC elements of each model, and how do they create value?

**RQ10**: Empirical Evidence and Methodologies  
What types of empirical evidence and methodologies have been used, and what are the evidence patterns?
