# Key Correlations and Patterns

## Overview

Cross-question patterns identified from closed-ended question analysis.

**Source**: `by_closed_ended_question/STATISTICS.md`

## Customer Type × Acquisition Channels

**B2B/Enterprise**:
- Direct sales: 75%
- Partners/channels: 61%

**B2C**:
- Product-led Growth: 68%
- Digital marketing: 54%
- Marketplaces: 39%

**Developers**:
- Product-led Growth: 80%
- Marketplaces: 60%

**Source**: `by_closed_ended_question/STATISTICS.md` (Q18 section)

## Company Role × Network Effects

**Two-sided platform** (Q16) → **Cross-sided network effects** (Q17):
- 76% correlation

**Standalone product** (Q16) → **Direct network effects** (Q17):
- 50% correlation
- 30% have no network effects

**Source**: `by_closed_ended_question/STATISTICS.md` (Q17 section)

## Marketplace Usage × Customer Type

**B2C** (Q04) → **App Stores** (Q19):
- 40.3% use mobile delivery
- 20.9% use Apple App Store
- 19.4% use Google Play

**B2B/Enterprise** (Q04) → **Cloud Marketplaces** (Q19):

## Multi-Channel Patterns

**3+ channels simultaneously** (Q18):
- 68% of models use multiple channels
- "Full-stack" acquisition: Product-led + Direct sales + Partners + Digital + Marketplaces
- "Partner-driven B2B": Direct sales + Partners
- "Digital-first growth": Product-led + Digital + Marketplaces
- "Viral growth": Product-led + Referral + Digital

**Source**: `by_closed_ended_question/STATISTICS.md` (Q18 section)
