# Key Findings Summary

## Overview

Consolidated key findings from the SLR analysis of 67 papers (2014-2024).

**Sources**: 
- `by_closed_ended_question/STATISTICS.md`
- `by_open_ended_question/analysis/CODES_COUNT_REPORT.md`
- Distribution JSON files

## Most Prevalent Business Models

1. **Platform business model** - 391 mentions (most cited)
2. **SaaS (Software as a Service)** - 289 mentions
3. **Mobile app market** - 279 mentions
4. **Freemium model** - 264 mentions
5. **Open source business model** - 188 mentions

## Revenue Models

- **Subscription**: 76.1% of studies (dominant)
- **Usage/consumption**: 41.8%
- **Transaction**: 32.8%
- **Advertising**: 19.4%
- **Perpetual license**: 16.4%

## Delivery Models

- **Multi-tenant SaaS**: 71.6% (dominant)
- **Mobile**: 40.3%
- **On-premises**: 26.9%
- **Desktop**: 16.4%

## Customer Segmentation

- **B2C**: 41.8%
- **B2B SMB**: 35.8%
- **Enterprise**: 7.5%

## Product Types

- **Application**: 89.6%
- **Platform**: 79.1%
- **Managed Service**: 35.8%
- **Infrastructure**: 13.4%

- **Technology evolution**: 83 mentions

## Key Patterns

1. **SaaS/Subscription dominance**: 76.1% use subscription, 71.6% use multi-tenant SaaS
2. **Hybrid models common**: Multiple product types, revenue sources, delivery modes
3. **B2B focus**: 53.7% target B2B, but many serve both B2B and B2C
4. **Platform evolution**: 46.3% are two-sided platforms with cross-sided network effects
5. **Mobile app stores**: 40.3% use mobile delivery, 20.9% use Apple App Store
