# Hierarchical Code Structure (Detailed)

## Business model characteristics

### Cost structure

- **Economies of scale**
  - Business model advantage from serving large customer base

### Customer retention

- **High switching costs**
  - Business model characteristic where customers face barriers to changing providers

- **Low switching costs**
  - Business model characteristic where customers can easily change providers

### Delivery model

- **Cloud delivery model**
  - Software distribution through cloud infrastructure and remote hosting

- **SaaS (Software as a Service)**
  - Software delivered as hosted service accessed over the Internet

### Model type

- **Hybrid business model**
  - Business model combining multiple approaches (e.g., both SaaS and traditional licensing)

### Pricing strategy

- **Cost-based pricing**
  - Pricing strategy based on vendor costs and cost accounting

- **Pay-as-you-go pricing**
  - Usage-based pricing model where customers pay for what they consume

- **Subscription-based pricing**
  - Recurring payment model for software access with monthly/annual billing

- **Value-based pricing**
  - Pricing strategy based on customer-perceived value rather than cost

### Revenue model

- **Data-driven monetization**
  - Business model generating revenue from user data and analytics

- **Freemium model**
  - Business model offering free basic version with premium paid features

- **Open source business model**
  - Business model based on open source software with various commercialization strategies

- **Perpetual licensing**
  - Traditional software licensing model with one-time payment for lifetime use

### Value creation

- **Marketplace model**
  - Business model facilitating transactions between multiple parties

- **Network effects**
  - Business model benefit where value increases with more users

- **Platform business model**
  - Business model creating value through ecosystem of external connections and data

### Value delivery

- **API-based business model**
  - Business model providing value through application programming interfaces

### Value proposition

- **Digital transformation**
  - Business model enabling digital transformation of traditional industries

- **Service-oriented model**
  - Business model emphasizing service delivery over product sales

- **Sustainability focus**
  - Business model integrating ecological sustainability into core operations
